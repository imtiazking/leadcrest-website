The Three-Step Process

Simple for you.
Rigorous behind the scenes.

01
You Define Your Ideal Buyer

We start with a short onboarding call where you tell us exactly who you want. Budget range, preferred locations (Downtown, Palm, Business Bay, JVC), property type — off-plan or ready, villa or apartment — preferred nationality mix, and timeline urgency. The more specific you are, the tighter we target.

This brief drives every sourcing and verification decision we make. It is live for the duration of your engagement and updated any time your focus shifts — new project launch, seasonal push, portfolio change. You stay in control without doing any of the legwork.

10-min onboarding call Custom targeting brief Location preferences Budget range filters Property type Nationality preferences
02
We Source, Score & Verify

Our system continuously monitors 14 behavioural and demographic signals across Dubai property portals, relocation platforms, expat financial communities, and our own inbound lead network. Every prospect is scored automatically — only those above our intent threshold are passed to the human verification team.

A trained LEADCREST qualifier then calls every shortlisted prospect. They confirm name, budget, timeline, current location, reason for buying, and availability to view. If the prospect hesitates, misrepresents budget, or fails to match your brief — they are rejected. You only see leads that passed every filter.

We also independently verify identity details — passport reference and UAE visa or entry status — before clearing any lead for delivery. Over 60% of raw enquiries are filtered out before you ever see them.

14 intent signals scored Human verification call Passport verified Budget cross-checked Visa status confirmed 60%+ rejection rate
03
You Receive the Dossier & Close

Verified leads arrive in your inbox or CRM within 48 hours — each one with a full buyer dossier. Name, contact number, email, confirmed budget, property preferences, timeline, intent score, and a plain-English summary of our verification call including what the buyer said about their motivation and requirements.

There is no cold calling from your end. You are reaching someone who has already confirmed they want to buy Dubai property, spoken to our team about it, and is expecting an agent to follow up. Your first call is warm. Your only job is to close.

Full lead dossier Delivered in 48 hours CRM or email delivery Verification call summary 100% exclusive to you Warm first contact

Intent Scoring

The 14 signals we track
before you see a single name.

01
Property Portal Activity

Frequency and recency of Dubai property searches on Bayut, Property Finder, and Dubizzle.

02
Search Query Specificity

Generic searches score low. Area-specific, budget-specific queries signal serious intent.

03
Relocation Forum Engagement

Activity on expat communities discussing Dubai housing, visa options, and lifestyle.

04
Mortgage Pre-Approval Status

Prospects who have sought mortgage advice or pre-approval are significantly higher intent.

05
Inbound Lead Form Submission

Prospects who voluntarily submitted interest via our own network and partner channels.

06
Visa & Residency Status

Whether the prospect holds a UAE visa, is applying, or is visiting with purchase intent.

07
Stated Purchase Timeline

Buyers indicating a decision window under 90 days are scored significantly higher.

08
Device & Engagement Depth

Time on listing pages, saved searches, and return visit frequency on property platforms.

09
Financial Content Consumption

Engagement with content about ROI, Dubai investment returns, and Golden Visa eligibility.

10
Off-Plan vs Ready Interest

Specific intent type helps us match buyers to the right agency and development portfolio.

11
Country of Origin

Nationality aligns with buying behaviour patterns, financing preferences, and visa eligibility.

12
Income Indicator Signals

Job title, industry, and employer data used to cross-validate stated budget plausibility.

13
Communication Responsiveness

Response rate to initial outreach and speed of engagement with our qualification team.

14
Repeat Search Behaviour

Prospects returning to the same listings or refining searches over multiple sessions show committed intent.
